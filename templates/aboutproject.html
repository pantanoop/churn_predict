üîç Dataset Features Description

The dataset used in this project is a customer churn dataset from a telecom company. It contains detailed information about individual customer profiles, their service usage behavior, contract details, and whether or not they have churned (i.e., discontinued the service). The primary objective of analyzing this dataset is to understand the factors that influence customer retention and churn, and to build predictive models that can identify customers at risk of leaving the company. Each row in the dataset represents a single customer and their associated features. These features include both demographic attributes such as whether the customer is a senior citizen or has dependents, and service-related attributes such as internet service type, online security subscription, and tech support availability. Additionally, billing-related features like monthly charges and total charges help analyze financial patterns in relation to churn behavior. The target variable in this dataset is Churn, which is a binary feature indicating whether the customer has left the service (Yes) or not (No). Understanding this outcome in correlation with the other features is crucial for developing effective customer retention strategies.
